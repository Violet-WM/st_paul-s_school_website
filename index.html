About Us

About Us

St. Paul’s Kinangop Academy is one of the best known schools in the country and numbers amongst its former pupils a very wide range of successful men and women. Situated on two sites at the Foot of Aberdare Ranges in Kinangop, its roots lie in the belief that education ecompases academic instruction, good morals and extra-currucular activities. It was founded in 1996 by the late Onesmus Waititu Thumbi and his family to uplift the education standards in Nyandarua County (Nyandarua District by then). Here at St Paul’s Kinangop Academy we believe that education is about so much more than excellent exam results, as important as morality and discipline are. Our we strive to be a beacon of inclusive excellence developing confident, tolerant and enthusiastic young people who enjoy working with others and are ready to influence the world.

MISSION STATEMENT

Empower learners with knowledge, skills and integrity needed to contribute and succeed as a responsible lifelong learner in a competitive global community.

VISION STATEMENT

We work to provide a stimulating learning environment which allows the learner to achieve full academic, social and moral potential imbued with Christian values
