Admissions

Join St. Paul’s School and be part of our family of excellence.

Admission Requirements

To join St. Paul’s School, applicants must submit:

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Previous academic transcripts or report forms
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs

Application Process

  1. Pick admission form from our office or download from the website.
  2. Submit the completed form with required documents.
  3. Attend an interview and entrance assessment (where applicable).
  4. Receive admission letter upon successful evaluation.

Why Choose Us?

At St. Paul’s School, we provide holistic education, a safe learning environment, and opportunities that prepare learners for future success. Our focus on academics, discipline, and character development makes us the best choice for your child’s growth.
